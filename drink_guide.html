Flavors

Coffee

Simply Coffee (unless its Cold Brew)

Tea

Latte

Diary Base with added espresso (more caffeine, is not coffee)

Macchiato

Diary base with layered espresso on top (more than latte)

Cappuccino

Dairy base with added espresso, then strongly mixed create foamy consistency

Americano

Water base with added espresso (more than latte)

Refresher

Juice concentrate with secondary liquid of choice mixed in.

Frozen Coffee

Blended caffeinated drink with iconic coffee flavor. Cream base and coffee syrup built in.

Coolata

Blended Drink with an emphasis on sweet, pretty much a smoothie.
